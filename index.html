  • Phone & fax systems

    Gone are the days of hiring someone to come run phone wiring all throughout your facility, leaving you with a line and not much else. Today, we use state-of-the-art VoIP phone technology. VoIP stands for “Voice over IP” and it’s basically phone-via-internet. It’s easier to install, configure, maintain, and–best of all–it’s more affordable (up to 50% more affordable!). you not only save on the costs of installation, calls themselves are significantly less expensive.


    With VoIP you can effortlessly customize your telephone system. Voicemail, messages, hold music–it’s all at your fingertips. You can even seamlessly transfer calls between practice locations, whether they’re next door, or in the next county.


    We set you up with a full suite of phone features. All the traditional stuff you’re used to, plus call hold, call transfer, call hunt conference calling, find/follow me, and auto-attendant phone menus. We can even have your recordings voiced by professional voice actors.


    Virtual faxing (also called IP faxing) is another feature of VoIP phone systems. Even in the age of email and instant communication, there are some instances when faxes are necessary. Without the need for paper or toner, you can send and receive faxes using the email account you already have with a hosted VoIP system.


    Switching to VoIP means having your cake and eating it too–everything you like about traditional phones, plus all the benefits of modern features.
